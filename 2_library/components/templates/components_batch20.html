Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our collections, services, and membership programs.

We partner exclusively with certified mills and tanneries that meet rigorous environmental standards. Our fabrics are either organic, recycled, or produced using closed-loop systems that minimize water and chemical usage. Every material is fully traceable from source to finished garment.

After joining our membership, you'll be paired with a dedicated stylist who will learn your preferences, lifestyle, and goals. Sessions can be conducted in-person at our showrooms or virtually. Your stylist curates personalized selections each season and is available for ongoing consultation.

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items with original tags attached. Members enjoy extended 60-day returns and complimentary exchanges. All return shipping is prepaid, and refunds are processed within 5 business days of receiving your return.

Yes, we believe in perfect fit. Couture and Haute members receive complimentary alterations. All other customers can access our alteration services at our showrooms for a nominal fee. Our in-house tailors ensure every piece fits flawlessly.

Our showrooms are located in New York, Paris, Tokyo, and London. Appointments can be booked directly through your member dashboard or by contacting our concierge. Walk-ins are welcome during select hours, though appointments are recommended for the full experience.
